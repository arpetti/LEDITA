The Learning Design Tool for Italian Language Teachers

Ledita is a free web application created for teachers of Italian as a second/foreign language. The aim of this application is to support the educational planning carried out by the teachers, proposing a simple, fast and intuitive design process.

With Ledita, teachers can create educational designs and make them visible to colleagues all around the world, to share educational experiences and learn from the dialogue and collaboration with other professionals.
